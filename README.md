### ✦ Los Cruceños Archive
 
> *"La necesidad de componer es la más difícil de resistir… La música es el único medio que realmente puede capturar los sentimientos de un momento."*
> — Necho Durán, Los Cruceños
 
Los Cruceños was founded by Antonio “Tony” Ledesma Fresquez and Onesino “Necho” Duran, Mexican musicians whose voice and compositions resonated in their community for decades. Their music captured the essence of an era, preserving stories and the spirit of New Mexico and its people.
 
This is a personal preservation project: digitizing, restoring, and archiving their original recordings so the music remains accessible for generations to come. Every recording has been carefully remastered from the original source material.
